Who are we?


Sinew is a language enhancement programme for students from high school starting with 9th grade to college graduates. Taught by Mr. Jeevan Pratap, the programme aims to bridge the learning gaps between what the student already knows and what is to be known to communicate in english effectively. It doesn't just help in polishing one's language but also makes you ready to face the selection process for companies.

Frequently Asked Questions


How do I make the payment?

We take payments offline. Once you register from the below form, we will contact you with further details.

Is there a refund policy?

No. Unfortunately, we don't have any such provision.

Does the programme guarantee a job or internship?

No, we don't guarantee any job or internship. What we can subjectively guarantee is to substantially improve your language skill which isn't just helpful in job hunt but also long run.

Is the course online?

No, this is an offline course as we believe courses like these are best taught that way. You can know the venue in the information section above.
